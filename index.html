PGRKAM Web

Welcome to the PGRKAM Web platform, your gateway to finding the perfect job and career opportunities. Our mission is to empower you with the information and resources you need to excel in your career journey. Discover Your Path: The "Udyog Saarthi App" is a transformative online platform dedicated to empowering Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) by enhancing their access to vocational training, job opportunities, and career development. This inclusive platform not only assists PwDs in creating personalized learning profiles but also provides crucial information about government and private sector job vacancies, exam preparation support, and awareness campaigns. By bridging the information gap and offering tailored resources, the app aims to significantly improve PwDs' employment prospects, ultimately fostering economic independence and a dignified life for this deserving community. How It Works: With our platform, you can create a customized learning profile that empowers you to enhance your skills, pursue education, and find the right support. Whether you're looking for job openings in the government sector or private companies committed to diversity and inclusion, we have you covered. We also offer application assistance services, including resume building, interview preparation, and workplace accommodations guidance.

Learning profile

Unlock Your Full Potential!

Welcome to your Learning Profile, a powerful tool designed to help you reach new heights in your career and personal development. At PGRKAM, we believe in empowering individuals with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed, and your Learning Profile is where it all begins.

Here's what you can do with your Learning Profile:

  • Personalized Skill Development: Tailor your learning journey to your unique goals and aspirations. Choose from a wide range of skill development programs and courses that match your interests.
  • Job Opportunities: Your Learning Profile helps us connect you with job openings that match your skills and preferences. Discover exciting career opportunities, gig jobs, and skill development initiatives.
  • Foreign Counseling: Interested in exploring international opportunities? Your Learning Profile provides access to foreign counseling initiatives, guiding you toward global career paths.
  • Real-time Geospatial Insights: We use cutting-edge technology to notify you of job openings and opportunities in your current location. Simply point your camera phone at a building or location, and relevant job notifications will appear on your screen.
